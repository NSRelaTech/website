## Session Fields

- **Topic:** NSRT Meetup — What neighborhood tools do residents need?
- **Template:** Brainstorming (`brainstorming`)
- **Goal:** Identify the most pressing neighborhood needs among Novi Sad residents and discover what tools would help build stronger community connections
- **Context:** NSRT (Novi Sad Relational Tech) is a community initiative for residents of Novi Sad, Serbia. "Relational technology" means tech that strengthens connections between people — contrast with platforms that extract data and attention. Principles: neighbor-to-neighbor (not citizen-to-government), village-scale (block, building, neighborhood), many small interoperable tools (not one monolithic platform), remix existing ideas, community sovereignty over tools and data.
- **Critical question:** What is the single biggest gap in your neighborhood life that a simple tool could address?
- **Cross-pollination:** Yes
